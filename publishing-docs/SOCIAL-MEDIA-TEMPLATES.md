## Tips for Promotion

1. **Timing**: Post during business hours (9am-5pm) in your target timezone
2. **Visuals**: Include screenshots or GIFs showing the plugin in action
3. **Engagement**: Respond to comments and questions promptly
4. **Cross-post**: Share across multiple platforms
5. **Follow-up**: Post updates, tips, and use cases over time
6. **Community**: Engage with Figma community, don't just promote
7. **Value**: Focus on solving problems, not just features
8. **Authenticity**: Share your story and why you built it
